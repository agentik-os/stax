  workspace has no programmatic surface.

## APPLY
1. Expose `window.stax` from the shell: getState() (serializable state), path(),
   find(text) → [{key,title}], open(key) deep-link, openSpace(spaceId),
   actions(panelId?) → the REGISTRY's [{id,label,kind}], act(actionId, panelId?),
   pin/unpin/close/focus, undo/redo. Read current ws through a ref (never stale);
   remove the global on unmount.
2. The agent surface (drawer) executes /commands through the bridge (/open,
   /actions, /run, /pin, /close, /undo, /redo) and answers with what it did.
3. Document the contract as a paste-ready prompt (M8 in agents.md): read
   getState() before acting, prefer act() over synthetic clicks, ids come from
   getState()/find(), never fabricated.

## VERIFY
- In the console: stax.find("account") returns keys; stax.open(key) opens the
  panel; stax.actions() lists the foot's actions; stax.act(id) runs one;
  stax.undo() rewinds it. The drawer answers /actions with the same list.
