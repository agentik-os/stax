# U-033: UX Wave 4 — wayfinding truth, first-run care, platform conventions

TARGET: {{TARGET}}
REFERENCE: frameword/apps/crm-specimen (24 vetted fixes from the five-lens panel)

## DETECT
- The home crumb CLOSES the root instead of focusing it; the "n pinned refs"
  crumb looks clickable but is inert; leaf AND focused crumbs both paint accent.
- Dynamic-entity panels (tables, pages, notes, tasks) carry bare-type eyebrows;
  long parents truncate the TYPE token out of the bar.
- The tour writes its "toured" flag on START (one Escape kills the only visible
  affordance) and narrates in 10px mono at the corner while acting on the stage.
- The push tier clips the panel foot (no min-height:0 on the push stage).
- prefers-reduced-motion covers a fraction of the animated surfaces; no
  pointer:coarse handling; every shortcut hint hardcodes ⌘; punctuation drifts.
- Press states dead (:active shadowed by later :hover), scroll surfaces off the
  house scrollbar law, bordered per-row capsules on the rail, boxed composers
  per group, terminal prompt unfocused on open, wheel users stranded.

## APPLY
1. WAYFINDING: home crumb = focus root + scroll home (⌥ rewinds), never close;
   pinned-refs crumb focuses the rail's first ref; exactly ONE accent crumb
   (focused; the leaf is bold ink); eyebrows compose parent › friendly-word
   (page/table/folder) and SPLIT so the parent ellipsizes, never the type.
2. FIRST RUN: the toured flag writes on COMPLETION only; tour narration rides
   the bottom-center .toast pill (subtitle size, role=status), crumb toasts
   suppressed while touring; tick 2400ms; the hands-on drill leads the
   overview; push stage gets min-height:0 so the foot is always reachable.
3. CONVENTIONS: reduced-motion lists EVERY entry-animated surface (functional
   infinite indicators excluded); pointer:coarse inflates hit areas to ~44px
   with tiled ::after insets; a keys module renders ⌘/⌃ vs Ctrl+ per platform;
   sections end with a period, leaves never; a plain vertical wheel translates
   the stage when nothing under the cursor scrolls natively.
4. MICRO: :active rules AFTER :hover (press-back renders); scroll surfaces
   adopt the thin themed scrollbar; per-row actions are quiet 24px icons
   revealed on hover/:focus-within (accent when active), never bordered
   capsules; one live composer at a time (ghost "+ Add" rows elsewhere);
   terminal foot prompt autofocuses when its panel leads; list snippets join
   blocks with " · " and end on a word boundary; theme-aware swatch rings.
